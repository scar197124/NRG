⚡ Welcome to NRG

NRG is your apartment-friendly home energy simulator. It helps you:

• Add and manage household devices
• Estimate your daily kWh, monthly usage, and cost
• Instantly see your biggest energy consumers
• Works with smart devices (smart plugs, switches, CT clamps, and more)

No meter? No problem. NRG puts the power back in your hands.

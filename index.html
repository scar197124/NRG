Basic Function

Start with the space you live in. NRG adds common built-in usage first; your personal devices come next.
Preset starting point: NRG adds common built-in appliances and baseline lights only.
  • Remove anything you do not have or do not use.
  • Empty receptacles/outlets are counted as 0 kWh until you add a plugged-in device.
  • TVs, laptops, routers, modems, consoles, and personal devices stay optional through Quick Add.

Add Quick Device
